Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 27, 2026

Welcome to ToolSnipe (“we,” “us,” or “our”). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit toolsnipe.com (the “Site”). Please read this policy carefully. By using the Site, you agree to the practices described below.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us and information collected automatically when you use the Site.

Information you provide:

  • Email address — when you sign up for our waitlist or set a price alert. We use this only to send you the communications you requested (launch notifications, price drop alerts). We do not sell your email address.

Information collected automatically:

  • Log data — when you visit the Site, our servers automatically record information including your IP address, browser type, operating system, referring URLs, and pages visited.
  • Cookies and similar technologies — we use cookies to understand how visitors use the Site and to improve your experience. See Section 4 below for details.
  • Usage data — we may collect aggregate, anonymized data about how users interact with the Site (e.g., which products are viewed most often) to improve our service.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To send you price drop alerts and launch notifications you explicitly requested
  • To operate, maintain, and improve the Site
  • To analyze usage patterns and understand how the Site is being used
  • To comply with legal obligations
  • To detect and prevent fraud or abuse

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for their own marketing purposes.

3. Affiliate Links and Third Parties

ToolSnipe displays product links to third-party retailers (such as Home Depot, Amazon, and Lowe's). These links may be affiliate links, which means we may earn a small commission if you make a purchase after clicking through — at no extra cost to you. This is how we fund the service.

When you click an affiliate link, you are leaving the ToolSnipe site and your interaction with that retailer is governed by their own privacy policy. We do not receive any personal data from these retailers about your purchases.

4. Cookies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to operate the Site and analyze traffic. Cookies are small text files stored in your browser. You can instruct your browser to refuse all cookies or to indicate when a cookie is being sent; however, some portions of the Site may not function properly without cookies.

We may use the following types of cookies:

  • Essential cookies — required for basic site functionality
  • Analytics cookies — help us understand how visitors interact with the Site (e.g., via Google Analytics or similar services). These are anonymized and aggregated.

5. Data Retention

We retain your email address for as long as necessary to provide the service you signed up for. If you unsubscribe from price alerts or the waitlist, we will remove your email from our active contact list. We may retain anonymized usage data indefinitely for analytics purposes.

6. Your Rights and Choices

You may opt out of receiving emails from us at any time by clicking the “Unsubscribe” link in any email we send, or by emailing us at toolsnipeapp@gmail.com.

If you are a resident of the European Economic Area (EEA), California, or another jurisdiction with applicable data protection laws, you may have rights including access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data. Contact us at toolsnipeapp@gmail.com to exercise these rights.

7. Data Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Children’s Privacy

The Site is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we learn that we have collected such information, we will delete it promptly.
